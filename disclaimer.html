CheapRazors.com earns affiliate commissions when you purchase through links on this site. We only recommend products we have genuinely reviewed and believe offer good value. Affiliate relationships do not influence our rankings — products are evaluated and ranked before affiliate links are applied.

Our rankings are locked before we look for affiliate programs. If we can’t find an affiliate program for a top-ranked product, it stays in the ranking anyway.

💰 Full Affiliate Disclosure

CheapRazors.com participates in affiliate advertising programs, including the Amazon Associates Program and other retailer and brand affiliate programs. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.

When you click a product link on this site and make a purchase, we may receive a commission from the retailer at no additional cost to you. These commissions help fund the cost of running this site, including product testing, research, and writing.

Affiliate links are marked with context (e.g. “See on Amazon”) throughout the site. The presence of an affiliate link does not mean the product is endorsed above alternatives — it simply means we may earn a commission if you choose to buy it.

⚖️ Editorial Independence

All product rankings, recommendations, and review scores on CheapRazors.com are determined independently by our editorial team, led by Tom Bradley. Rankings are based on hands-on testing across four weighted criteria: value for money (35%), shave quality (30%), blade longevity (20%), and accessibility — whether the product is available without a subscription or long shipping wait (15%).

Advertisers, brands, and affiliate partners have no ability to purchase placement, influence rankings, or request changes to editorial content. If a brand contacts us about a product, it is evaluated on the same criteria as any other product.

We do not accept payment for reviews. Products are purchased at retail or supplied as press samples; where press samples are used, this is noted in the review. Press samples do not affect scores.

📊 Accuracy of Information

Prices shown on CheapRazors.com are accurate at the time of writing but change frequently. Always verify the current price on the retailer's site before purchasing. We update pricing information regularly but cannot guarantee real-time accuracy.

Shave quality scores, blade counts, and cost-per-blade figures are based on our own testing and publicly available product specifications. Individual results will vary based on hair type, skin sensitivity, shaving technique, and how frequently blades are replaced.
