1. Information We Collect

CheapRazors.com collects only the information necessary to run the site:

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Analytics data is used to improve site content — never for targeted advertising.

3. Affiliate Links

When you click a product link on CheapRazors.com, an affiliate cookie may be set by the retailer. If you subsequently purchase the product (within the retailer's cookie window, typically 30 days), we may earn a commission. The price you pay is identical whether you use our link or go directly to the site.
